<p>A couple relating two Types which asserts that one type is a subset of the other. <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Note - This is the standard super-sub-type relation where the sub-type can either be a proper sub-type or an improper sub-type of the super-type. (An improper sub-type of a type is the type itself. A proper sub-type of a type is not the type itself.) This relation holds between types, where every instance of the sub-type is also an instance of the super-type. Hence the type, Humans, is a sub-type of the type, Animals, because every instance of a Human is also an instance of Animal.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Examples:<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>* VW Golfs is a subtype of Cars<br/></p><p>* People over 2m tall is a subtype of People<br/></p><p>* Types of Pump is a subtype Types of Equipment<br/></p>
