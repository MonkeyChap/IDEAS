<p>A couple that asserts one (part) Individual is part of another (whole) Individual.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Note - This is the standard whole-part relation where the whole can either be a proper part or an improper part of the part. (An improper part of a whole is the whole itself. Whereas a chocolate chip would be a proper part of a cookie, only the entire cookie is an improper part of itself)<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Examples:<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>* My head is part of Me<br/></p><p>* January 2004 is part of 2004<br/></p><p>* The opening shot of the First World War is part of the First World War<br/></p><p>* The Border of France is part of France<br/></p><p>* The Border of France and Belgium is part of the Border of France<br/></p><p>* The Border of France and Belgium is part of the Border of Belgium<br/></p>
