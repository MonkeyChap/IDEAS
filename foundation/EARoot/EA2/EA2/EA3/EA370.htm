<p>IDEAS takes a very simple approach to representing Measures. The Representation pattern is specialised for NumericMeasureRepresentation. This approach does not claim to be anything more than a naming mechanism for Measures.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Note: Purists would argue that the Measure should first be mapped onto a number which itself should be represented numerically. The set of mappings would then represent the scale - e.g. the kilogram scale - and so give a more detailed representation of units of measure. This does not mean that what is shown here is incorrect - it is just a simplification.<br/></p>
