<p>This diagram shows how temporal boundaries are represented in IDEAS. There is no subtype of Individual for temporal boundaries - rather it is the role that in individual plays in respect to another (in this case, it is a particular type of temporal part).<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>A temporalBoundary is a temporalWholePart which asserts that an Individual is the temporal boundary of another Individual.<br/></p>
