<p>IDEAS provides a simple model for numbers. The model follows the broad consensus in mathematics that numbers are sets (Types) , but does not follow the usual mathematical approach that the specialisation goes from RealNumber to RationalNumber to Integer to PositiveInteger.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Note1: This should not be confused with numerals or other numeric representations. These are the concepts of the numbers themselves, not their representations<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Note2: The specialisation hiearchy shown here is by no means accepted by all pure mathematicians and philosophers of mathematics<br/></p>
